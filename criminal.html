Criminal Law

Advocate Anil Kumar specializes in high-stakes criminal defense and strategic litigation. Our practice ensures the protection of fundamental rights through rigorous legal representation across all judicial levels.

Core Expertise & Legal Protections:
  • Constitutional Protections: Enforcement of Article 22(1) and the right to legal counsel during all stages of arrest and trial.
  • Bail Jurisprudence: Expertise in Anticipatory Bail (Section 438 CrPC) and Regular Bail to secure personal liberty.
  • Statutory Defense: Comprehensive litigation for matters involving FIR Quashing, Section 138 NI Act, and specialized criminal acts.
  • Trial Advocacy: Meticulous cross-examination and representation in sessions trials and appellate proceedings.
