ABOUT THE ADVOCATE

Advocate Anil Kumar is a seasoned legal professional practicing at the Supreme Court of India and the Punjab and Haryana High Court. With a reputation for strategic legal architecture and a stubborn commitment to justice, he specializes in navigating complex litigation with a result-oriented approach.

Known for meeting strict judicial deadlines and maintaining a sharp focus on procedural perfection, his practice spans across Criminal Defense, Matrimonial Disputes, and High-Stakes Civil Litigation.
